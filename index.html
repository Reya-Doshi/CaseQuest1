THE DIAGNOSIS

India's FPO Paradox

Over 8,000 FPOs have been created with institutional support. They are excellent social institutions, but structurally weak businesses.

  • Only 66% are operational
  • Majority are grant-dependent
  • Limited access to credit

1. Margin Expansion

Most FPOs fail because they only aggregate. We introduce primary processing immediately. Standardised quality commands premium pricing.

  • Bulk input procurement (5-10% cost savings)
  • Reduce intermediaries through direct processing

2. Market Access Strategy

We flip the model. Instead of "Produce then Sell", we use a Buyer-First approach. We sell solutions, not just raw produce.
